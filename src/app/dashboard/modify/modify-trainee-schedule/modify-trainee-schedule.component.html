The input format must be: Trainee number,Attendance date,Time in,Time out,Type
  • Trainee number, whose trainee training schedule you wish to set
  • Attendance date, in general date format
  • Time in, when the trainee must enter
  • Time out, when the trainee must leave
  • Type of the attendance, must be 'Room', 'Secretariat', or 'Rest'
  • Schedule Room, where the schedule should be held on
  • For example,
    • T000,2011-11-30,07:19,17:20,Secretariat,610
    • T999,12/31/2011,11:00,11:45,Rest,610
